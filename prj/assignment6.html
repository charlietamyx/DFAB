3D modeling

Sketching

I started 3d modeling my nametag with this sketch, all the tools that i used are in the top left corner.

  1. Firstly, I drew a rectangle with the 2 point rectangle tool 65mm by 25mm
  2. Then I used the Fillet tool on the edge to create an arch of 6mm radius
  3. I then offset the rectangle by 2mm inwards and added the circle and my name

Extruding

I then proceeded to extrude my sketch

  1. Firstly, extruded the inner rectangle by 3 mm
  2. Then I extruded the edge by 4.5mm and the words as well
